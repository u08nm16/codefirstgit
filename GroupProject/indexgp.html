About Us

Cats on Catnip is a cat cafe located downtown. We host book club meetings on the third Saturday each month! The book theme for each upcoming month will be anounced during the book club meeting and posted shortly after. Members will be given an assortment of themed treats and goodies each month! Our cafe has an asortment of books that can be bought or borrowed to read at the cafe with a cat by your side. Honestly, what's better than cats, books and a hot chocolate?

April's Book Theme

The Long Way to a Small Angry Planet

The Long Way to a small angry planet, by Becky Chambers, is a light-hearted and diverse story of space travel. A perfect read if you're looking for a road trip through space adventure!

Our Cats!

Jiji

Jiji is named after the black cat from the Studio Ghibli film "Kiki's Delivery Service." She is a very shy kitty and kind of afraid of people, but loves cuddles anyway. Can be won over with tuna.

Hamish

A fluffy rascal who wants all the attention. Hamish is known to meow at customers until they notice him. Watch out: he will shove you coffee off the table.

Peanut

Peanut is our resident snuggle-buddy. He is 3 years old and just wants to nap. He's a bit of a flirt, rubbing against people's legs. Be careful not to trip over the lil' love bug.

Skye

Skye is a 4 year old beauty queen. She's the most photogenic of our cats. That beautiful coat of fur has got to be shown off, afterall.

Snowball

Snowball is the youngest member here at Cats on Catnip at only 4 months old! He is full of energy with his youth, and will pounce on your shoelaces. He's quickly becoming a favorite at the cafe!

Bear

Bear is 1 year old and was found outdoors, so we gave him a home at Cats on Catnip! He has the personality of a hunter and loves to play with all the cat toys. He also has the tail of a squirrel that he likes to show off.

Io

Pronounce eye-oh. Named after the a Greek goddess who was turned into a cow by Zeus. Io is the newest member here at Cats on Catnip and is 7 months old, so she is full of energy and ready to pounce on your shoelaces!

Natalie

Also known as Nattie Cat. She's a bit tempermental and super sassy. Nattie runs around after meal time, or as we like to say "she gets the zoomies." Immediately after her spurt of energy, all she wants to do is nap.
