Dodie at Barrowland

15 March, 2019

My most recent expedition was to Glasgow to see Dodie in Concert. The concert was amazing, Dodie has such a lovely voice. The vibe fit my aesthetic perfectly. The fairy lights and pink spotlights are goals.

Glasgow Science Center

16 March, 2019

As a chemistry student, this was a must see in Glasgow. It's one of the nicer science centers I've been to, it's fun for all ages. There were some nice photo ops throughout the building as well!

Lochness

This is going to be a day trip to Inverness to see Loch Ness. I've been in Scotland for 3 years and have yet to venture here. I know that the Loch Ness monster is just a myth, but it's still fun to pretend! Urquhart Castle is next to the loch, so I plan to get the medieval experience amongst the castle ruins.
